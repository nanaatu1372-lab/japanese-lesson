🏢 企業向け — For Companies / Employers

Companies that provide Japanese training to foreign employees can receive up to 75% of costs from the government.

Human Resource Development Support Subsidy

最大75%
補助率 / Coverage

従業員への計画的な職業訓練（日本語教育を含む）に対して、研修費用の最大75%が支給されます。
Up to 75% of training costs covered for systematic employee training including Japanese language education.

厚生労働省

人材確保等支援助成金（外国人労働者就労環境整備助成コース）

Foreign Worker Employment Environment Support Subsidy

最大80万円
上限 / Max amount

外国人従業員の就労環境整備（日本語教育含む）に1制度につき20万円、最大80万円が支給されます。
¥200,000 per measure (max ¥800,000) for improving workplace environment for foreign workers, including Japanese language education.

For SMEs in Tokyo. This 30-hour course qualifies directly for this grant.

📌 申請の流れ / Application Steps

1
ハローワークで「支給要件照会」を行う / Visit Hello Work to check your eligibility
2
厚労省指定講座を受講・修了する / Complete the designated training course
3
修了証明書をもってハローワークに申請 / Apply at Hello Work with your completion certificate
✓
給付金を受け取る / Receive your benefit payment!
⭐ 重要：このコースが給付金の対象指定講座になるには、講師（垰田先生）が厚労省への申請が必要です。申請は年2回（4月・10月）受付。
Note: For this course to qualify, the instructor must apply to the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare. Applications accepted twice yearly (April & October).
